Hi, I'm a Software Developer.

I am currently working as a Web Developer in Newcastle-Upon-Tyne. Over the past few years I have worked with a wide range of technologies, most recently specialising in JavaScript.

JavaScript

I have been using JavaScript for over 2 years on single page applications. I have thoroughly enjoyed using JavaScript as it is a rapidly changing area with a variety of new tools. My most recent experience is in Backbone with Marionette and EmberJS. However, I have also worked with Angular, Knockout, Node, Gulp, Grunt and RequireJS - to name a few!

iOS

I have a keen interest in iOS development. I been involved in projects at work and at home. I have worked on automating an iOS build process, a little bit of automated testing, setting up app certificates, development, ad hoc distribution using TestFlight and releasing to the App Store.

C# and ASP.NET

Whilst studying at Teesside University, I used C# and the .NET framework to create multiple desktop and web applications. During my final year of University I developed an ASP.NET MVC3 application, which included a database created using Entity Framework code first development and JSON services for an iPad application.

I have also started to blog!

I would like to share my experiences and let you know what I am working on. As I have gained more experience, I find fun in picking up new technologies and believe I can help other people learn whilst creating a reminder for myself :).
